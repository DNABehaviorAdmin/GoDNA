The Beginning

It started with a realization. As a Chartered Accountant in Australia, Hugh saw a pattern: brilliant financial plans failing because of ignored human behavior. He realized that "money problems are actually people problems."

Driven by this insight, he left traditional accounting to pioneer a new field. In 2001, he founded DNA Behavior in Atlanta, building the world's first system to scientifically measure and align human behavior with financial and business performance.
